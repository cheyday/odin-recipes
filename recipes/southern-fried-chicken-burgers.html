Southern Fried Chicken Burger

Designed by Freekpik

Description

This burger is filled with succulent chicken, chipotle sauce, pickles and more.

If you have been hanging out for a home-cooked southern fried chicken burger that taste just as good as the burger shop, look no further. You won't be disappointed.

Ingredients

  • 400g Coles Southern Fried Thigh Fillet Burgers
  • 2 Brioche Buns
  • Coles Special Burger Pickles
  • Coles Creamy Chipotle Sauce
  • Coleslaw
  • Cos Lettuce
  • Fries, to serve

Steps

  1. Heat a tsp of olive oil in a frying pan, on medium heat. Cook chicken until each side is golden brown and the inside is white.
  2. Prepare the other ingredients, choosing as much or a little as you like.
  3. Spread chipotle sauce on the brioche buns. Top with cos lettuce and pickles.
  4. Place chicken on top of pickles, add coleslaw and serve.
