Lasagna

Designed by Freekpik

Description

Lasagna is a pasta dish made primarily of pasta sheets, cheese, beef mince and a tomato based sauce.

This particular recipe is easy to make, dairy and soy free, and perfect for the little ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 x box of Lasagna sheets
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 3 x 420g cans of Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion, diced.
  • 2 tbs Minced Garlic
  • Vegan Mozzerella, sliced.
  • 1 tbs Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil and Oregano
  • Optionial: Shredded Vegan Cheese for top layer

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees c Fan Forced, or 200 degrees c without.
  2. Split oil into a frying pan and saucepan and heat on medium/low.
  3. Split onion between the two pans, add a tbs of garlic to each pan, stir until mixed and cook until the onion is soft.
  4. Add mince to frying pan, break up and cook until brown.
  5. Meanwhile, add tomatoes to saucepan, stir to combine and bring to boil.
  6. Once boiled, reduced to a simmer, add herbs and stir. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  7. Add tomato mixture to mince and stir. Take off heat.
  8. Using a large rectangular oven dish, spoon enough mince and tomato mixture to cover the bottom.
  9. Top with enough lasagna sheets to cover the mince.
  10. Repeat steps 7 and 8 until the dish is half full. Add mozzerella between mince mixture and the lasagna sheets.
  11. Continue with layers until dish is full, ensuring the top layer is only the mince mixture.
  12. Place in oven, cook for 30-40 minutes or until top is golden brown and lasagna sheets are soft.
